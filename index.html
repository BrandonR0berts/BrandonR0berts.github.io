About Me

Currently I am a first year computer games programmng student studying at Staffordshire University who, for a period of 7 months during 2019/2020, worked as a programmer for a local mobile games company, Curioso Games. Whilst at this company I worked in a small team to develop two of the games listed in this portfolio: Marbles, and Rituals. Whilst at Curioso we used Unity extensivly, taking advantage of the rendering, audio and animation systems contained within the engine in order to create the engaging and interesting games shown above.

For the past 4 years I have been programming projects in C++, using versions of visual studio that range from 2015 to 2019. During this time my programming knowledge and abilty has improved dramatically. This has been through me tasking myself with increasingly more difficult projects over the years. These were in the form of game jams, retro game recreations or, most recently, developing projects for my degree.

The goal of undertaking a degree in computer games programming is mainly to increase my knowledge within the area of games programming. Along with also to increasing my employability overall. This is so that, when I graduate, securing a dream job is a more realistic prospect.
