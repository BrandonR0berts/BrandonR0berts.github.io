About Me

Since 2016 I have been programming projects that range in target area and scope, aiming to learn as much as I can about the area of computer games programming as I can. Since starting, I have worked at a local games company, Curioso Games, completed multiple games jams, started, and completed the first year of, a computer games programming course at Staffordshire University.
